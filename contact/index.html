Common questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What is The Bay Club in Bayside, NY?

The Bay Club is a gated condominium community at 1 and 2 Bay Club Drive in Bayside, Queens, NY. The community features two high-rise residential towers on 13 landscaped acres beside Little Neck Bay, with a 25,000 sq ft Leisure Club, 24-hour concierge and security, and on-site retail and dining.

Is The Bay Club a condominium or cooperative?

The Bay Club is a condominium (condo) community, not a cooperative (co-op). Residents own their individual units outright.

How many units does The Bay Club have?

The Bay Club has over 1,000 residences across two towers at 1 Bay Club Drive and 2 Bay Club Drive in Bayside, Queens, NY 11360.

What amenities does The Bay Club Leisure Club include?

The Leisure Club spans approximately 25,000 sq ft over three levels and includes an indoor pool with a retractable glass roof, fitness center, tennis and pickleball courts, indoor basketball court, golf simulator, sauna, steam rooms, community room, movie lounge, library, kids club and playground, and a restaurant and bar.

Is The Bay Club a gated community?

Yes. The Bay Club is a fully gated community with 24-hour staffed entry, doormen, and concierge service at both residential towers.

How do I contact The Bay Club management?

The Bay Club can be reached at (718) 631-5214. Property management is provided by Douglas Elliman Property Management. Residents can also access the full contact directory through BuildingLink.

Where is The Bay Club located?

The Bay Club is located at 1 and 2 Bay Club Drive in Bayside, Queens, NY 11360, in the Bay Terrace neighborhood near Little Neck Bay.

What transit is near The Bay Club Bayside?

The Bay Club is served by the QM2 and QM20 express buses to Manhattan. The Bayside LIRR station (Port Washington Branch, ~30 min to Midtown) is a short drive away. The Clearview Expressway (I-295) and Cross Island Parkway are minutes from the community.

Does The Bay Club have parking?

Yes. The Bay Club has indoor garage parking and outdoor lots for residents, including EV charging stations at both towers.
