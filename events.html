Events @ The Bay Club!

Here are some of the previous events held at The Bay Club. Below is a listing of past events and dates — each item links to a PDF flyer with the full event details and promotional materials.

Saturday Night Dance Party

An evening dance party with a resident DJ, open dance floor, light bites and a cash bar — a relaxed way for neighbors to meet.

Comedy Night at The Bay Club

A comedy night showcasing local comedians in an intimate setting; ticketing and seating information is available in the event flyer.

Sunday Leisure Club Brunch

A family-friendly brunch featuring buffet service, kids' activities and optional add-ons for adults.

Golf Simulator Skills Clinic

Small-group sessions in the golf simulator with instructor-led coaching and practice drills for a range of skill levels.

Family Pool & Splashpad Day

A family pool day with music, games and snacks by the pool and splashpad, with optional swim activity sign-ups.
