Leisure Club

The “Jewel” of The Bay Club

Built into the heart of the community, the three-level Leisure Club is positioned as The Bay Club’s signature differentiator—a modern, luxury amenity stack that mirrors resort wellness clubs. The structure below pulls together the key elements from the current HOA site and Swim & Fitness pages into a cleaner, story-driven layout.

The Leisure Club combines a fully equipped fitness center, multi-use courts and dedicated studios across roughly 25,000 sq ft — offering classes, private training, and year-round recreational play for residents.

Indoor pool with retractable roof

The centerpiece of the club is the glass-enclosed pool—used year-round for laps, family swim and events. In warmer months the roof opens to the sky; in winter the enclosure keeps the space bright and protected.
