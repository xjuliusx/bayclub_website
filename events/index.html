Upcoming Events

The Bay Club hosts a year-round calendar of community events for residents of Bayside, Queens. Held at the Leisure Club, events range from live music performances and themed dinner nights to fitness classes, holiday celebrations, and family activities at 1 Bay Club Drive. The Bay Club community event program is run by the Leisure Club management team and open to residents and eligible guests. New events for the Bay Club community are added throughout the season — check back regularly for the latest updates.
