Leisure Club

The "Jewel" of The Bay Club

Built into the heart of the community, the three-level Leisure Club is positioned as The Bay Club's signature differentiator—a modern, luxury amenity stack that mirrors resort wellness clubs.

With roughly 25,000 square feet of amenity space, the Leisure Club brings together a fully equipped fitness center, multi-use courts, and dedicated studios for classes, private training, and year-round recreational play. Few condominiums in Bayside, Queens match the depth of the Bay Club Leisure Club. From early-morning lap swimming in the glass-enclosed pool to evening tennis and pickleball matches, the club is built for active, social living. Bay Club residents also benefit from on-site dining, a full-service hair salon, valet dry cleaning, and community spaces — including a movie lounge, library, and event room — all within steps of both East and West towers. The Leisure Club is designed to be the social and wellness hub of the community, supporting a wide range of activities for residents of all ages and interests.

Indoor pool with retractable roof

The centerpiece of the club is the glass-enclosed pool—used year-round for laps, family swim and events. In warmer months the roof opens to the sky; in winter the enclosure keeps the space bright and protected.

  • Above-grade indoor pool with glass retractable roof
  • Lap-friendly configuration (olympic size)
  • Dedicated kids splashpad for younger swimmers

Fitness

Fully equipped gym with free weights, machines and cardio zones plus personal training and small-group sessions.

  • Free weights, selectorized machines and functional rigs
  • Cardio zone with treadmills, bikes and rowers
  • Personal training and small-group classes

Tennis & pickleball courts

Dedicated court time for tennis and pickleball, with flexible setup for practice, social matches, and league-style play.

  • Tennis courts with pickleball conversion lines
  • Open play windows and resident reservation slots (reserve via Residents Area)
  • League play, clinics and coaching availability

Basketball court

Full-court indoor basketball space for pick-up games, resident leagues, and all-season training.

  • Indoor full-court play in all weather conditions
  • Outdoor basketball court also offers full-court play
  • Open gym windows and scheduled resident use (reservation info)
  • League and youth program opportunities

Studios

Dedicated studios for yoga, cycling, barre and group fitness classes, with flexible scheduling for all levels. Class schedules are posted in Mind & Body website/app (view in Residents Area).

  • Yoga, Pilates, spin and functional group classes
  • On-demand class library and instructor-led sessions

Golf Simulator

A state-of-the-art indoor golf simulator offering full-swing tracking, virtual course play, and private bays for lessons and year-round practice—available for residents and guests.

  • Launch monitor with ball-flight and club-data analysis
  • Private bays available by reservation (bookings in Residents Area)
  • Instructor-led lessons and club-fitting services
  • Leagues, events and casual play options

Social life & events

Beyond fitness, the Leisure Club supports a calendar of entertainment and resident events across the club.

  • On-site restaurant and bar with event catering
  • Live entertainment and themed event nights
  • Movie lounge, card room, billiards and library

Social clubs & resident activities

Residents can join classes and community programming throughout the week, including aqua aerobics, bridge and mahjong meetups, book clubs, and hosted socials.

  • Tai chi, stretch and chair yoga
  • Aqua aerobics and lap swim hours
  • Bridge, mahjong and book clubs
  • Kids Club, children's playroom, outdoor playground and splashpad
  • Weekday socials and events

On-site conveniences

The lower-level arcade adds everyday convenience to life at The Bay Club, with essential services and dining just steps from home.

  • On-site convenience store for everyday essentials
  • Full-service hair salon and beauty services
  • Valet dry cleaner with pick-up and delivery
  • Casual restaurant / café for dining and events
